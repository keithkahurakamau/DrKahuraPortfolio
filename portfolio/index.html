About Dr. James Kamau Kahura

40+ Years Experience

Dual Expertise in Nephrology & Internal Medicine

Dr. James Kamau Kahura is a highly respected Consultant Physician and Specialist Nephrologist serving the communities of Murang'a and Kiambu counties from his practice at Mayo Clinic in Kenol. With over four decades of dedicated medical service, Dr. Kahura has established himself as the leading authority in kidney care and internal medicine in the region.

His comprehensive approach combines cutting-edge medical knowledge with compassionate, patient-centered care. Whether managing chronic kidney disease, hypertension, or complex internal medicine conditions, Dr. Kahura is committed to achieving the best possible outcomes for every patient.
